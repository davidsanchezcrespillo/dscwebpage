--- extends: base.j2 title_ending: "| Elearning paper" default_block: content --- #Construyendo la web de un Instituto de Educaci贸n Secundaria This paper describes the making of the website for the [Emili Darder High School](http://www.iesemilidarder.com), in Palma de Mallorca, during the academic year 2007-2008. The goal is to make the website a tool to spread the activities of the school and a support tool for teaching and administration. It's important that all members of the school are able to publish and update the contents of the website, without depending on a single person. To make this possible, Content Management Systems (CMS) have been installed, both publication-oriented ([SPIP](http://www.spip.net)) and teaching-oriented ([Moodle](http://www.moodle.org)). The paper explains the steps taken, the reason for every technological choice, the structure of the contents and the training for the school teachers. Finally, the benefits of having this system are detailed, compared to the old approach. (Please note: The paper is in Spanish, no translation available).
